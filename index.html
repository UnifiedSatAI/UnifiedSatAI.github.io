Project Overview

Satellite imagery is critical for environmental monitoring, urban planning, and disaster response. However, real-world images often suffer from clouds, noise, low resolution, or missing data.

Our project develops a unified deep learning benchmark that evaluates models for both restoration and generation tasks on satellite images. It provides a standardized platform to compare performance, test new algorithms, and accelerate research in remote sensing AI.

Project Goals

  • Create a standardized dataset for satellite image restoration and generation.
  • Implement baseline deep learning models for evaluation.
  • Provide metrics and visualizations to benchmark model performance.
  • Enable researchers and students to easily test and compare new models.
