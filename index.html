INTRODUCTION

Satellite imagery is a critical tool for monitoring the Earth, supporting applications such as environmental analysis, agriculture monitoring, urban planning, and disaster response.

However, real-world satellite images are often degraded by cloud coverage, atmospheric noise, and limited spatial resolution. These challenges reduce the usability of satellite data and make reliable analysis difficult.

Our project introduces a Unified Deep Learning Benchmark for Satellite Image Restoration and Generation, designed to systematically evaluate modern AI models for tasks such as cloud removal, super-resolution, and image reconstruction. By bringing multiple restoration tasks, datasets, and evaluation metrics into one framework, we aim to provide a clear and standardized way to compare models and accelerate research in satellite image enhancement.
