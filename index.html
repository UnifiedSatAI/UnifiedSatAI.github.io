UnifiedSatAI

Unified Deep Learning Benchmark for Satellite Image Restoration and Generation

A unified framework for evaluating satellite image restoration and generation models with robust benchmarks, datasets, and interpretable results.

Introduction

Satellite imagery plays an important role in environmental monitoring, agriculture, and disaster response.

However, satellite images often suffer from cloud coverage, low resolution, and noise.

Our project builds a unified benchmark for evaluating satellite image restoration and generation models.
